About Easy Migration

The Easy Migration ETL tool is a software that is designed to bring the power of ETL to the business professional who does not come from a computer science background. Easy Migration focuses on taking the necessity for code out of the ETL process, empowering business users to move common data objects like csv files into powerful database systems like Snowflake.
  • Business Intelligence Analysts - anyone who knows enough about SQL to empower analytics but don't have the background in computer science to interface with modern database code.
  • Analytics Professionals - people who are constantly being held back in their analytics capabilities because they cannot perform necessary tasks on data extracts without them being in a modern database.
  • Data Engineers - data engineers are constantly bombarded with ad-hoc data requests and do not always have time to write stable ETL code for these one-off requests. Easy Migration can handle this for your data engineers in an easy to use format.
  • Data Scientists - are you an R wiz who can manipulate stats every which way but struggle to deal with data loading once your results have been generated? Easy Migration can help alleviate these ETL pains with an easy to use UI and fast tutorials.
    • We decided to create Easy Migration based on many shared conversations about the difficulties surrounding the ETL and how bussiness professionals are so many times shackled by needing too much code in order to perform a simple ETL process. Easy Migration strives to make ETL accessible to any business professional in a no-code, easy to use format.
    Our software engineering team brings 20+ years of software experience to the table with a wide variety of experience in data engineering, machine learning, and full stack engineering. The three lead developers for Easy Migration have worked in a multitude of industries including financial services, hospitality and travel, telecommunications, energy, consulting, and technology. In each of these fields, a common problem of hard to access ETL arose in all of their jobs, bringing them together to build a solution for democratizing ETL.
